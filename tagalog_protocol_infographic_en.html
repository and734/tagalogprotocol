A Vision of Manila, 2195

A Stratified Organism

In 2195, Manila is divided. Two worlds coexist in a delicate and tense balance: one of impeccable logic and the other of imperfect memories, a soul divided between the sky and the earth.

SKYGRID

The Upper Levels

  • Existence governed by logic and protocols.
  • Imposing towers of semantic surveillance.
  • Every thought and action is monitored.
  • A world of absolute order and control.

MIDZONE

The Lower Levels

  • A living archive of glitches and memories.
  • A chaotic, poetic, and human soul.
  • The language of gestures, silence, and empathy.
  • A world of imperfection and resilience.

The Central Conflict: Logic vs. Poetry

The true battle is not fought with weapons, but with ideas. Skygrid and Midzone represent two opposing philosophies, visualized here based on their fundamental values.
